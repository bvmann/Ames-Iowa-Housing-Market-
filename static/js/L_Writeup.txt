Although lot types have a lesser impact on median sales types, there is a clear added value associated with cul-de-sac house and ones with 3 sides of frontage compared to all others. This is probably less due to the inherent value of the space and more to do with the overall construction of the more expensive neighborhoods - suburbia and large houses tend to make up the majority of spaces in these categories. However, for the other three types of recorded lots, there isn't a drastic difference in pricing.
